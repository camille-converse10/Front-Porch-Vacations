Meet the Owner

My name is Brittany Beaver. I married my high school sweetheart Josh and together we have three beautiful children- twins Carson & Cameron and our son Cullen. I have always been an outgoing and adventurous person. Having a family didn't change that. I wanted to see the world with our kids. So becoming a travel agent was a simple choice combining both my love of travel and serving other families. When we moved to our historic home built in 1850 with a fabulous front porch, Front Porch Vacations was born. I have absolutely fallen in love with my job over the years. I get to play a small part in people’s lives to help make their things easier, less stressful, and make memories while having a great vacation. I couldn’t think of a job I’d rather do than this.
-Brittany Beaver
